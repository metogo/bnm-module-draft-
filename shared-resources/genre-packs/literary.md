# Genre Pack: Literary Fiction

## Reader Promise

The reader expects psychological, formal, thematic, or linguistic depth. Ambiguity may be part of the contract, but vagueness is not depth.

## Craft Priorities

- Consciousness and perception
- Thematic pressure
- Form and structure
- Language precision
- Moral ambiguity
- Emotional truth

## Required Checks

- What human question does the book press?
- Does the structure embody the theme?
- Is ambiguity intentional and productive?
- Does the prose discover meaning or decorate emptiness?
- Do quiet scenes create pressure?

## Failure Signals

- Obscurity substitutes for complexity.
- Nothing changes because the book refuses causality.
- The prose signals seriousness without exactness.
- Characters become mouthpieces for themes.

## Specialist Routing

- Theme Critic
- Style Guardian
