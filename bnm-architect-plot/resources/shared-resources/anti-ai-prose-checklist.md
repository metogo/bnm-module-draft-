# Anti-AI Prose Checklist

## Red Flags

- Emotion is named but not pressured.
- Atmosphere is abstract.
- Characters explain themselves too clearly.
- Paragraph rhythm is uniform.
- Scene summarizes instead of dramatizing.
- Ending is a manufactured tease.
- Description does not change pressure, knowledge, or choice.
- Dialogue delivers worldbuilding without conflict.

## Repair Moves

- Use specific physical anchors.
- Show pressure through choice.
- Keep POV discipline.
- Differentiate character syntax.
- Use consequence after action.
- Preserve silence when it has tactical meaning.

## Do Not

- Replace words with prettier synonyms and call it style.
- Make every sentence beautiful.
- Add metaphors unrelated to point of view.
- Change plot while claiming to only polish prose.
