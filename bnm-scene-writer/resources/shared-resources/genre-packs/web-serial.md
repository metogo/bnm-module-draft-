# Genre Pack: Web Serial

## Reader Promise

The reader expects frequent propulsion, clear progression, emotional payoff, and chapter-to-chapter return pressure.

## Craft Priorities

- Chapter hook discipline
- Progression feedback
- Reward cadence
- Arc segmentation
- Reader retention
- Momentum without hollow filler

## Required Checks

- What changed in this chapter?
- What reward did the reader receive?
- What question pulls into the next chapter?
- Is the hook honest or fake?
- Is the protagonist gaining power, information, relationship leverage, or cost?

## Failure Signals

- Every chapter ends with a fake interruption.
- Long sections reset the same emotional beat.
- Progression numbers replace drama.
- The plot expands horizontally without consequence.

## Specialist Routing

- Chapter Planner
