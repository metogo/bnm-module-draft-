# BNM Project Structure for Chinese Novel Projects

Recommended folder:

```text
docs/novel/
  author-intent.md
  genre-positioning.md
  story-bible.md
  character-bible.md
  world-bible.md
  plot-architecture.md
  style-guide.md
  continuity-ledger.md
  foreshadowing-register.md
  revision-log.md
  chapters/
  reviews/
```

## Canon Status

Use:

- 已确认 canon
- 工作假设
- 需要作者拍板
- 已废弃

## Minimum Chapter Loop

1. Chapter brief
2. Scene cards
3. Continuity pre-check
4. Draft
5. Style pass
6. Ledger update

## Hard Rules

- Do not draft without chapter purpose.
- Do not canonize major assumptions silently.
- Do not enter a world rule without cost.
- Do not accept a character without current pressure.
- Do not leave foreshadowing without payoff status.
