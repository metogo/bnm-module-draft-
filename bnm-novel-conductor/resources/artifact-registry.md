# Artifact Registry

## Core

- `author-intent.md`: author purpose, taste, boundaries, approval requirements.
- `genre-positioning.md`: genre contract, reader promise, comparable works.
- `story-bible.md`: core promise, dramatic question, story engine, constraints.
- `character-bible.md`: characters, arcs, relationships, voice seeds.
- `world-bible.md`: rules, costs, institutions, material life, research.
- `plot-architecture.md`: acts, turns, midpoint, crisis, climax, ending.
- `style-guide.md`: voice, prose rhythm, POV, avoid list.
- `continuity-ledger.md`: facts, timeline, knowledge, objects, foreshadowing, reveals.

## Chapter Level

- `chapter-brief.md`: purpose, starting state, ending state, scene cards.
- `draft-chapter.md`: controlled chapter draft.
- `chapter-diagnosis.md`: revision diagnosis.

## Revision

- `developmental-edit-report.md`: structural problems and rewrite priorities.
- `pacing-map.md`: tension and release across chapters.
- `contradiction-report.md`: canon and logic issues.
- `revision-log.md`: approved changes.

## Rule

If a decision affects future chapters, it must be recorded in an artifact or marked as unapproved.
