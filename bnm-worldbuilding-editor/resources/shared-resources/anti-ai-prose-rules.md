# Anti-AI Prose Rules

Avoid:

- Summary where a scene is needed.
- Explaining emotion in abstract terms.
- Stock atmosphere phrases.
- Everyone speaking in one cadence.
- Convenient revelations.
- Mechanical chapter hooks.
- Ornate sentences that do not alter pressure, image, or rhythm.
- Theme speeches.

Prefer:

- Physical anchors.
- Pressure shown through choice.
- Character-specific diction.
- Information control.
- Consequence after action.
- Deliberate silence.
- Specific sensory fields tied to point of view.
