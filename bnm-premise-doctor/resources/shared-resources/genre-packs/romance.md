# Genre Pack: Romance / Emotional Relationship

## Reader Promise

The reader expects emotional progression, romantic or relational obstacles, meaningful vulnerability, and an ending that honors the relationship contract.

## Craft Priorities

- Relationship arc
- External and internal obstacles
- Intimacy escalation
- Misunderstanding discipline
- Chemistry through pressure
- Choice, vulnerability, and cost

## Required Checks

- Why are these people drawn to each other specifically?
- What prevents the relationship from resolving too early?
- What emotional risk must each person take?
- Does intimacy change the plot or only decorate it?
- Does the ending satisfy the promise: HEA, HFN, tragic, or ambiguous?

## Failure Signals

- Attraction replaces relationship development.
- Misunderstanding could be solved by one obvious conversation.
- Romantic beats repeat without escalation.
- One character exists only to validate the other.
