# Genre Pack: Fantasy / Xuanhuan

## Reader Promise

The reader expects wonder, power systems or mythic pressure, meaningful cost, and a world whose rules generate conflict.

## Craft Priorities

- Magic or cultivation rules
- Cost and limitation
- Rank, institution, lineage, or cosmic order
- Mythic resonance
- Escalation without infinite power inflation

## Required Checks

- What can power do and not do?
- What does advancement cost?
- Which institution controls access?
- How does the protagonist's path disrupt the order?
- Does scale increase while personal stakes remain alive?

## Failure Signals

- Power solves every problem.
- Rules change whenever convenient.
- Lore delays story.
- The protagonist wins because a new ability appears at the exact moment needed.
