# BNM Build World Bible

## Metadata

- code: bnm-build-world-bible
- title: Build World Bible
- description: Creates setting rules, costs, institutions, research boundaries, and conflict pressure.

## Purpose

Build a world that constrains characters and generates plot. This workflow turns setting ideas into operational rules for future chapters.

## Roles Used

- Worldbuilding Editor
- Story Architect
- Genre Strategist
- Continuity Editor

## Inputs

- User setting notes
- `story-bible.md`
- `plot-architecture.md`
- `character-bible.md`
- Genre pack

## Flow

1. Define what the world rewards, punishes, forbids, and hides.
2. Define rules, costs, exceptions, and who knows each rule.
3. Define institutions and what pressure they exert.
4. Connect material life and history to present conflict.
5. Mark forbidden moves and research questions.
6. Register future-facing canon into continuity.

## Output

- World bible
- Rules and costs
- Setting conflict map
- Research questions
- Continuity entries

## Gate

Lore is not approved unless it changes access, risk, choice, conflict, or consequence.

## Reader-Impact Standard

If the world fails, describe it in user impact: readers will feel the setting is decorative, powers are too convenient, or rules change whenever the author needs them to.
