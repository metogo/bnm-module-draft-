# Genre Pack: Historical / Alternate History

## Reader Promise

The reader expects period pressure, material specificity, social constraint, and believable historical consequence.

## Craft Priorities

- Era-specific constraint
- Material life
- Language boundary
- Social rank and institution
- Research discipline
- Alternate-history divergence logic

## Required Checks

- What could this person plausibly know, own, say, or do in this period?
- What institution constrains them?
- Which modern assumptions are leaking into the scene?
- Does historical detail create pressure or only decoration?
- If alternate history, what consequences follow from the divergence?

## Failure Signals

- Modern psychology in period costume without intent.
- Research facts inserted as lecture.
- Social constraints disappear when inconvenient.
- Historical setting can be removed without changing the story.
