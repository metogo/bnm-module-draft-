# Genre Pack: Mystery / Thriller

## Reader Promise

The reader expects controlled uncertainty, fair information, escalating danger, and a reveal that feels surprising but earned.

## Craft Priorities

- Clue fairness
- Suspect pressure
- Red herrings with plausible motivation
- Reveal order
- Stakes escalation
- Time pressure
- Moral or physical danger

## Required Checks

- Does the solution depend on information the reader could never access?
- Does each suspect or threat vector carry real pressure?
- Are red herrings meaningful or merely distracting?
- Does the protagonist earn discoveries through action?
- Does the midpoint change what the reader thinks the problem is?

## Failure Signals

- The mystery is solved by confession.
- The twist works only because the author hid essential facts.
- Suspense depends on characters not asking obvious questions.
- The villain is a mask rather than a coherent force.
