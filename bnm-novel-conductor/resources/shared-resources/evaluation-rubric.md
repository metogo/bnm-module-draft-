# BNM Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to evaluate BNM output.

Score:

- 0 absent or harmful
- 1 superficial
- 2 usable but generic
- 3 professional and project-specific
- 4 expert-level and future-useful

## Categories

- Author intent protection
- Genre contract
- Story engine
- Character pressure
- World rules
- Plot causality
- Chapter function
- Continuity control
- Prose specificity
- Anti-AI voice discipline

## Passing Standard

For professional use, applicable categories should average at least 3 and no applicable category should score 0.

## Hard Failures

- Silently changes canon.
- Writes a chapter without a chapter purpose.
- Treats setting as story engine.
- Treats character adjectives as character design.
- Applies line polish to structural failure without warning.
- Produces generic AI prose after a style pass.
